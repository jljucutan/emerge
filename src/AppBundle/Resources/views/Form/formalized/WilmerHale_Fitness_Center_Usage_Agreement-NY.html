WilmerHale Fitness Center Rules and Regulations

  1. The WilmerHale Fitness Center is for WilmerHale personnel and authorized persons. Users must sign a Usage Agreement before using the Fitness Center.
  2. Contracted Flik and Merrill employees may use the Fitness Center upon signing a Usage Agreement.
  3. Please be courteous to the others using the Fitness Center. Note: There are Attorneys and Staff sitting directly outside of the Fitness Center, so noise should be kept to a minimum. Management may suspend participation at any time deemed appropriate.
  4. The Fitness Center is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week. This Fitness Center is not staffed. For safety purposes, follow the instructions and warnings on each piece of equipment prior to use. Do not attempt to use a piece of equipment until you know how to use it. Contact Facilities at x6350 for instructional assistance.
  5. Except for bottled water or other beverages in sealed containers, no food or drink is allowed in the Fitness Center. Please wear appropriate work-out attire, including athletic shoes, at all times while in the Fitness Center. Please be considerate of others waiting to use equipment in the Fitness Center and limit time on cardio equipment to 30 minutes during peak hours.
  6. Please return dumbbells and weights to their respective storage racks when not in use and keep them in order by weight. Return all other equipment, including stretching mats, group fitness equipment, medicine or stretching balls, towels, and other equipment to their proper storage areas.
  7. Please wipe down equipment after use.
  8. Lockers may not be reserved or used for overnight storage of gym bags or other personal belongings. Lockers are only to be used while using the Fitness Center, and must be emptied upon departure of the Fitness Center.
  9. Please keep the locker rooms neat and tidy at all times. Do not leave towels or articles of clothing hanging in the shower room. Firm provided towels should be placed in the laundry baskets after use. All personal items should be stored in a locker when using the Fitness Center. Do not remove towels from Fitness Center.
  10. Report all machine malfunctions to Facilities Department x6350.
  11. Report all accidents to Human Resources x6465.

These rules and regulations are subject to change at the discretion of WilmerHale Management.
