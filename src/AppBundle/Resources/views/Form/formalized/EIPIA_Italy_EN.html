EMPLOYEE INNOVATION AND PROPRIETARY INFORMATION AGREEMENT (the “Agreement”)

I hereby acknowledge, understand, and agree that:

  1. Confidential Information

    1. During my employment I will have access to confidential information and trade secrets concerning the business, operations, processes and affairs of the GE entity that employs me and/or other GE companies (together the “Company”), and its suppliers, customers, agents and employees which is commercially sensitive and which, if disclosed, may cause significant damage to the Company (“Confidential Information”).

    2. I shall not directly or indirectly (except as authorised or required in the proper course of my duties or as required by law), either during my employment or at any time after its termination (howsoever arising), use or disclose to any person, company or other organisation (and shall use my best endeavours to prevent the publication or disclosure of) any Confidential Information or any information in respect of which the Company (as defined in my contract of employment) owes an obligation of confidentiality to a third party which may come to my knowledge during my employment or otherwise.

    3. This restriction shall not apply to any information that is already in, or comes into, the public domain other than through my direct or indirect unauthorised disclosure, solely or through any third party, and will not prevent me from making a protected disclosure (which should be made pursuant to “How to raise an integrity concern” of the Company’s Integrity Policy - Annex 1).

    4. I understand that nothing herein prevents me from reporting potential violations of law to relevant government authorities.

  2. Intellectual Property

    1. All Intellectual Property Rights subsisting in or attaching to anything created by me (also in cooperation with other people) in the course of my employment with the Company and/or in the course of any special projects or duties shall belong to and vest in the Company absolutely to the fullest extent permitted by law and I hereby assign and transfer to the Company all such Intellectual Property Rights, except for the right to be named as the author.

    2. I undertake, at the request and the expense of the Company, to execute all such further documents and to do such other acts as may be necessary to vest any such Intellectual Property Rights in the Company absolutely and I hereby assign by way of present assignment of future copyright all copyright in any copyright works produced or originated by me in the course of my employment to the fullest extent permissible by Italian law.

    3. For the purposes of this Clause B, Intellectual Property Rights means all patent rights and utility model rights, copyright, design rights, trade mark and service mark rights, database rights, topography rights, trade names, domain names, trade secrets, know-how and any other intellectual property rights and other rights or interests in or relating to any of the rights listed immediately above (including, in particular, rights to apply for a registered protection of any such rights), whether or not they are registered, wherever in the world they arise or exist for the full period of their existence including any period of extension or renewal, and they may be exercised by the Company in all media and formats whether now known or hereafter devised.

    4. Any Intellectual Property will be notified and disclosed by me to the Company in an appropriate manner promptly upon its creation.

    5. I undertake that, at the Company's expense and upon request (whether during or after the termination of my employment), I will execute such documents, make such applications, give such assistance and do such acts and things as may be necessary to enable the Company to enjoy the full benefit of this Clause B. This will include the giving of assistance or advice (including giving evidence if so required) in connection with:

      1. the prosecution of any applications for the registration of;
      2. any proceedings brought to prevent the infringement of; and/or
      3. any proceedings concerning or affecting the validity of,

      any Intellectual Property Rights.

    6. Immediately upon the termination of my employment or earlier at the Company's request, I will deliver up to the Company all Intellectual Property Rights, and all underlying data and/or documents relating thereto, in my possession, custody or power in which the Company has rights by virtue of this Clause B.

  3. Privacy - Data Protection

    I acknowledge that my personal information will be treated in accordance with GE's Employment Data Protection Standards and I hereby confirm that I have read, understood and expressly approved and agreed in writing to the GE Employment Data Protection Standards and the Company’s Uses of Employee Information set out at “Annex 2” on <$client.env.FormField.date.HTML>, in full compliance with the provisions of Legislative Decree 196 of June 30, 2003, by means of an express subscription on the INFORMATION AND REQUEST OF CONSENT PURSUANT TO THE PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION LAW form which is hereby attached at “Annex 3”.

  4. Governing Law

    This Agreement and its attachments shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Italy and is subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Italian courts.

  5. Severability

    This Agreement supersedes any other provisions in any other agreement that I may have signed regarding or regulating my employment that differ. Any part of this Agreement that cannot be modified or interpreted to avoid being invalid shall have no effect. All other parts of any other agreement not in conflict shall remain valid and enforceable.
