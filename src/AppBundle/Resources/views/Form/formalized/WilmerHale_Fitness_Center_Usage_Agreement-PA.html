WilmerHale Fitness Center Rules and Regulations

  1. The WilmerHale Fitness Center and Scooters are for WilmerHale personnel and authorized persons. Users must sign a Usage Agreement before using the Fitness Center or Scooters.
  2. Contracted Merrill employees may use the Fitness Center upon signing a Usage Agreement.
  3. The Fitness Center is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week. This Fitness Center is not staffed. For safety purposes, follow the instructions and warnings on each piece of equipment prior to use. Do not attempt to use a piece of equipment until you know how to use it.
  4. Except for bottled water or other beverages in sealed containers, no food or drink is allowed in the Fitness Center.
  5. Please wipe down equipment after use with sanitizing wipes.
  6. Lockers may not be reserved or used for overnight storage of gym bags or other personal belongings. Lockers are only to be used while using the Fitness Center, and must be emptied upon departure of the Fitness Center.
  7. Please keep the locker rooms neat and tidy at all times. Do not leave towels or articles of clothing hanging in the shower room. Firm provided towels should be placed in the laundry baskets after use. Do not remove towels from Fitness Center.
  8. Report all machine malfunctions to Jannelly Sussman Jannelly.Sussman@wilmerhale.com.
  9. Report all accidents Natalie Fong in Human Resources x714-5055.

These rules and regulations are subject to change at the discretion of WilmerHale Management.
