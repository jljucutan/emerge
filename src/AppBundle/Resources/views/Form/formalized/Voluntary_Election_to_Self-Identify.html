VOLUNTARY ELECTION TO SELF-IDENTIFY TO PARTICIPATE IN AFFINITY GROUP ACTIVITIES AND OTHER DIVERSITY PROGRAMS.

If you are interested in participating in affinity group activities and other diversity programs, we invite you to complete this form. Your participation in these activities is voluntary. If you do complete this form, the information you submit will be shared with the Diversity Committee on an identified basis so that you may receive invitations to and notices about events sponsored by affinity groups. If you do not complete this form, you will not receive notices about events sponsored by affinity groups. (Please note, however, that even if you decide not to complete this form, you will still receive firm wide distributions about diversity and inclusion programs.) Your decision whether or not to complete this form will not have any impact on your employment or subject you to adverse treatment.

If you have questions about this form, or would like additional information regarding the firm’s diversity efforts and affinity groups, please feel free to contact the Legal Personnel and Development Department.
