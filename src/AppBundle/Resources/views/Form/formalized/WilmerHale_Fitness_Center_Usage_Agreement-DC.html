WILMERHALE FITNESS CENTER USAGE AGREEMENT

I hereby agree that in exchange for my access to and use of the WilmerHale Fitness Center (“Fitness Center”) at 1801 Pennsylvania Avenue NW, Washington, DC, I will comply with the following terms:

I acknowledge that it is my own responsibility to decide when and how to use the Fitness Center, to exercise caution and my own judgment when I am using the Fitness Center, and to forego using the Fitness Center in appropriate circumstances.

WilmerHale Fitness Center Rules and Regulations

  1. The WilmerHale Fitness Center is for WilmerHale personnel only. WilmerHale must have a signed Usage Agreement from you on file before programming your security badge to allow access and use of the equipment in the Fitness Center.
  2. Please be courteous to the others using the Fitness Center. Management may suspend participation in the event of behavior injurious to the enjoyment of the Fitness Center or health of other persons.
  3. Please return dumbbells and weights to their respective storage racks when not in use and keep them in order by weight. Return all other equipment, including stretching mats, group fitness equipment, medicine or stretching balls, and other equipment to its proper storage area.
  4. Lockers may not be reserved or used for overnight storage of gym bags or other personal belongings. Lockers are only to be used while using the Fitness Center. Lockers will be checked at 12 midnight each night by WilmerHale Security, and materials stored in a locker will be removed and stored in the Fitness Center management office. Material not claimed in 10 days will be discarded.
  5. Please keep the locker rooms neat and tidy at all times. Do not leave towels or articles of clothing hanging in the shower room. All personal items should be stored in a locker when using the Fitness Center.
  6. Report all machine malfunction to the WilmerHale Fitness Center Management.
  7. The Fitness Center is staffed from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. Monday through Friday, except for firm holidays. For safety purposes, follow the instructions and warnings on each piece of equipment prior to use. If you do not know how to use a piece of equipment, please ask for assistance. Do not attempt to use a piece of equipment until you know how to use it.
  8. Report all accidents to the WilmerHale Fitness Center management if the accident occurs during staffed hours. After staffed hours, notify WilmerHale Security at 202-247-4911..
  9. Except for bottled water or other beverages in sealed containers, no food or drink is allowed in the Fitness Center or locker rooms. Please wear appropriate work-out attire, including athletic shoes, at all times while in the Fitness Center. Please be considerate of others waiting to use equipment in the Fitness Center and limit time on cardio equipment to 30 minutes during peak hours. Please wipe down equipment after use.
  10. Do not lend your security badge to anyone so they may enter the Fitness Center. If you forget your security badge, see WilmerHale Security at the 1875 front desk for assistance.

These rules and regulations are subject to change at the discretion of WilmerHale Management.
