d. never share your login credentials with anyone else;
e. ensure any wireless network is secure;
f. store any necessary papers and equipment safely and securely;
g. use only a Company managed device for your work and shut down & restart the device at least once / week, to allow installation of patches to maintain security compliance;
h. consider carefully prior to printing any document containing confidential information or personal data and ensure it is either sent securely to the right destination or secured safely at your home and destroyed when no longer required.
